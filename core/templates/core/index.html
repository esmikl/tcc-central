What is TCC-Central?

TCC-Central is a community-driven forum and chat platform built for open discussion, creative expression, and connecting with like-minded people. Share posts on boards, chat in real-time, upload reels, and customize your profile.

Features

  • Discussion boards with replies and likes
  • Discord-style real-time chat with channels
  • Direct messages
  • Instagram-style reels with uploads
  • Customizable profiles with banners, avatars, and anthems
  • /play command for YouTube audio in chat
